calc(at, *args, **kw)[source]¶

Calculates all descriptors of this type in the Atoms object, and gradients if grad=True. Results can be accessed dictionary- or attribute-style; ‘descriptor’ contains descriptor values, ‘descriptor_index_0based’ contains the 0-based indices of the central atom(s) in each descriptor, ‘grad’ contains gradients, ‘grad_index_0based’ contains indices to gradients (descriptor, atom). Cutoffs and gradients of cutoffs are also returned.
