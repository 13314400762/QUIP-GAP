Units and physical constantsÂ¶

This module holds a collection of our units and conversion factors. We use Angstroms (), eV, and femtoseconds (fs).

  • Length: Angstroms, a.u. * BOHR
  • Energy: eV, a.u. * HARTREE
  • Time: fs
  • Mass: = a.u. * HARTREE * AU_FS**2 / BOHR**2
